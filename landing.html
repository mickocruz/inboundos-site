Do I need to film everything myself?

Yes. You film, we handle everything else: positioning, scripting, AI agents, review, strategy, and the lead conversion layer. You show up on camera. We build the machine around you.

How much time does this take per week?

One 60-minute 1:1 call per week, plus one monthly filming session you batch in advance. Everything else is async.

I'm not technical. Will I be able to use the AI agents?

They're built specifically for you. Type a one-line prompt and get a full script in your voice. We build it, we hand it over, you run it from day one.

We already tried ChatGPT. Why would this be different?

ChatGPT is a blank tool. We build the entire operating layer around it: your company database, your voice data, your custom agents, your batching workflow, your review process, your conversion sequences. The power is in what surrounds the AI.

Will the content actually sound like me?

Yes. We train the system on your calls, posts, frameworks, offers, objections, and stories. Then we review scripts and hooks before they go live, so the output sounds like you on your sharpest day.

What if I already have an editor or VA?

Even better. We give them a clearer machine to operate: content pillars, scripts, batching rhythm, review standards, and CTA flow. They stop guessing and start executing against a system.

Do I keep everything after 90 days?

Yes, all of it. The agents, workflows, prompt library, content calendar, strategy, review standards, and DM sequences. That's the whole point of installation. It lives inside your business.

Why not just hire a content agency?

Agencies charge $3K–$10K/month and keep the system when you leave. We build custom AI agents and workflows inside your business, and you own them after 90 days. Pay once, run forever.

What if it's not working?
